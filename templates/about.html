Our Story

Chandrika Jain Degree Mahavidyalaya

Located in Borda, Kalahandi district of Odisha, our college is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality higher education to students.

We offer undergraduate programs in Arts, Science, and Commerce streams. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and values for success.

With experienced faculty, well-equipped classrooms, comprehensive library, and supportive environment, we bring out the best in every student.
